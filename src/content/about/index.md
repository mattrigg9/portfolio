### Why I build
There are so many skilled programmers out in the world, but only a fraction truly appreciate the delicate relationship that a user has with their machine. This relationship fascinates me. This relationship is why I love to design and develop applications.

A well-built application should anticipate a users need before they even need to think about it, offering an experience that feels magical. I like to think of a well designed application like an airport. No matter where the user is, they should have enough context and spatial awareness to orient themselves and know what must be done next. Providing a user with this sense of control enables them to expertly navigate the application, no matter the size of scope.

### What I build
Want to get technical? I specialize in writing scalable and modern Javascript. I've worked extensively with popular rendering frameworks like React and Vue.js. I have intimate experience building semantic and thoughtful CSS frameworks, enabling developers to build complex use cases with ease.

As a certified AWS Solutions Architect, I have extensive experience in the AWS ecosystem. I have built and designed platforms based on a serverless microarchitecture platform that have scaled to millions of users. I have helped companies migrate their infrastructure to the cloud, saving thousands of dollars in operational upkeep costs.

This is the stuff I thrive on. My roots may run deep but my motives are simple; I build applications that people love to use.
