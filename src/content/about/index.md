### Why I build
There are so many skilled programmers out in the world, but very few really appreciate the relationship of a user's interaction with an application. This relationship is more important than many realize. Good interaction design so delicate that it can even influence a user subconsciously. Understanding and mastering this relationship of interaction absolutely fascinates me. This is why I love to build.

A well-made application predicts a users need before they even think about the next step, offering a seamless experience that just feels "right". I like to think that a well designed application is much like an airport. No matter where the user is, they have all the context they need to orient themselves and know what's next. Providing a user with this sense of control enables them to expertly navigate with little to no effort, no matter how large or complex.

### What I build
Want to get technical? I specialize in writing scalable and modern Javascript and TypeScript, built natively or using popular rendering frameworks like React and Vue.js. For front end web applications, I have deep-seated experience building semantic and thoughtful CSS frameworks, enabling developers to build complex use cases on top of the foundation with ease.

I love to build more than just front end experiences. My 6+ years of experience in AWS (Amazon Web Services) and a certification as an AWS Solutions Architect has given me deep and intimate knowledge of the cloud giant; not only all of its capabilities, but all of its quirks too. With over 210+ services, it's critically important to know which service is most appropriate based on a business's use case.

The world of cloud computing can offer so much for a business these days. Using the cloud, I have designed and built flexible systems based on serverless microarchitecture platforms that scale to millions of users while keeping costs low. Still stuck with in-house servers? I have migrated multiple on-premises infrastructures to the cloud, saving hundreds (and sometimes thousands) of dollars in operational upkeep costs.

Whether it's application development or cloud infrastructure design, this is the stuff I thrive on. My roots run deep but my motives are simple; I love to build applications that people enjoy using.
