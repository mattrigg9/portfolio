I build applications that people love to use. 
 
Thoughtful interactions in an application can be so delicate that it can even influence a user subconsciously. Understanding and mastering this relationship of interaction fascinates me. This is why I love to build.

A well-made application predicts a users need before they even think about the next step, offering a seamless experience that feels organic. A well designed application should be like an airport. No matter where the user is, they always have the right level of context to orient themselves and know where to go next. Providing a user with this sense of control enables them to expertly navigate with little to no effort, no matter how large or complex.

### What I build
I specialize in writing scalable, enterprise-ready TypeScript and Javascript, built natively or with rendering frameworks like React and Vue.js. I have created semantic and thoughtful code patterns that serve millions of users and scale across an entire organization.

Through my 7+ years of experience in AWS (Amazon Web Services), I have intimate knowledge the cloud and its best practices. Whether is performance, scalability, security, cost-efficiency, or operational excellence, I can design the best architecture for your use case.

Using the cloud, I have designed and built flexible systems based on serverless microarchitecture platforms that scale to millions of users while keeping costs low. Still stuck with in-house servers? I have migrated multiple on-premises infrastructures to the cloud, saving hundreds (and sometimes thousands) of dollars in operational upkeep costs.

Whether it's application development or cloud infrastructure design, I live to build. My roots run deep but my motives are simple; I build applications that millions of people love to use.
